Nick's Favorite Movies!

My favorite movies of all time

The Dark Knight

I love how the story is told. It's got great music, and the Joker is a fantastic villain.

The Blues Brothers

This is the movie that taught me to be a musician. Or rather, taught me how to approach gigging and playing music with less of a serious attitutde.

Star Wars: Return of the Jedi

This is such a great ending to the trilogy. It ties up the stories really well, reveals some long term motives and secrets, and is very fun to watch.
